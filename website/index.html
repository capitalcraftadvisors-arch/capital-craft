About Capital Craft

Solar financing,
finally simplified.

India's solar market is growing fast — but financing infrastructure hasn't kept up. Customers struggle to get loans. EPCs chase documents manually. Lenders can't trust unverified installers.

Capital Craft fixes this. We are the trusted middleware layer — standardizing EPC verification, digitizing the customer loan journey, and giving lenders the clean, verified data they need to deploy capital with confidence.

We are not a lender. We are not an EPC. We are the infrastructure layer that makes everything work.
